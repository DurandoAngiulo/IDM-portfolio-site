Star Wars Vector Art

This was another assignment for my Design for Media 1 class. The goal was to choose a photo from a movie/show/real life, and trace over it with shapes of different greyscale values. I chose a frame that stood out for me from the Disney show, “The Mandalorian”. Check out the before and after pictures!
