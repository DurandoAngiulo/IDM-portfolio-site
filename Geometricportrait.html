Geometric Portrait

This was a color exercise I did for my Design for Media 2 class. I took a portrait image of something I liked (I used a still of Han Solo), and traced over it it with triangular shapes of varying sizes over the colors of the image. After doing this, I then filled all the shapes in with colors of the majority pixel type in the set triangle area, creating the image you see now. Its a neat effect right?
